# Sample Circuit Problems for Testing

Use these in the **Ask** tab to verify the tutor is working correctly.

## Ohm's Law
1. A 4.7 kΩ resistor has 9 V across it. Find the current and power dissipated.
2. What resistance is needed to limit current to 20 mA from a 5 V supply?

## RC Circuits
3. An RC circuit has R = 10 kΩ and C = 100 µF. Find the time constant, cutoff frequency, and voltage at t = 1 s after a 5 V step.
4. Design a low-pass RC filter with a cutoff frequency of 1 kHz.

## RL Circuits
5. An RL circuit has R = 100 Ω and L = 10 mH. Find the time constant and steady-state current for a 5 V source.

## RLC / Resonance
6. Series RLC: R = 50 Ω, L = 10 mH, C = 1 µF. Find f₀, Q, and bandwidth.

## Op-Amp
7. Design an inverting amplifier with gain = -10 using Rin = 1 kΩ.
8. A non-inverting op-amp has Rf = 100 kΩ and R1 = 10 kΩ. Vin = 0.5 V. Find Vout.

## KVL / KCL
9. A loop has a 12 V source, a 3 kΩ and 1 kΩ resistor in series. Find current and voltage drops across each resistor.

## Transistors
10. BJT with hFE = 100, VCC = 12 V, RC = 1 kΩ. Find IC(sat) and the base current required for saturation.

## Logic Gates
11. Explain the operation of an XOR gate and draw its truth table.
12. Design a circuit using NAND gates only that performs the function of an AND gate.

## Diodes
13. A silicon diode has Is = 1 nA. Find the diode current at a forward voltage of 0.65 V at room temperature.
